I am a PhD Scholar at University of Brescia, Italy. As a scholar, my research interests are mostly focused on Corporate Social Responsibility, Human Resource Management, Business Ethics, Cross-Cultural Study, Strategy and Innovation and Leadership. I have always had an interest in projects that are interdisciplinary and which also foster a broad, social-environmental and political dialogue; I have published many papers in renowned journals, but I have also presented at conferences on CSR, Leadership and on sustainable performance. While my interests are vast, I have always found literary studies to satisfy my intellectual curiosity and provide a meaningful methodological foundation. Currently, I am working with International scholars with Nottingham Trent University, UK, University of Sharjah, UAE,University of Brescia, Italy and Quaid e Azam University, Pakistan to broaden my horizon.
